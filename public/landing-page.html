What Is This All About?

Zamboni is a community project built to bring NHL 10, 11, 14 and Legacy's (RPCS3) online multiplayer back to life. Complete with new servers, stats, and player support. Developers can join the effort or view the full source code on GitHub.

NHL 10 & 11 - Online Revival

NHL 10 and NHL 11 defined an era of online hockey on consoles. Their official servers are long gone, but Zamboni brings back core online functionality. From ranked matches to player stats rebuilt to work on modern setups through RPCS3.

NHL 14 & NHL Legacy Support

NHL 14 and NHL Legacy Edition were among the last classic-feeling NHL titles before the series fully shifted direction. Zamboni aims to preserve and partially restore online features where possible, keeping these games playable beyond their original lifespan.

Open Source & Preservation

Zamboni is built in the open with long-term preservation in mind. Server logic, tooling, and documentation are freely available for anyone interested in reverse engineering, game preservation, or community-run online services. Contributions, forks, and experimentation are all welcome.
