Frequently Asked Questions

What is Zamboni?

Zamboni is a community-driven project restoring online multiplayer for classic NHL games on RPCS3 - including NHL 10, NHL 11, NHL 14 and NHL Legacy Edition.

How do I play online on PC?

Join our Discord and follow the setup guide. You'll need RPCS3 and a legally obtained copy of the game before connecting to the servers.

Is this affiliated with EA?

No. Zamboni is a fan-made project and is not affiliated with or endorsed by EA or EA Sports.

Can I contribute?

Yes. Zamboni is open source - developers can contribute via GitHub.

Where can I see statistics?

The statistics page displays player rankings, match history and server activity.
